Plan Your Financial Freedom with a Coast FIRE Calculator

The Coast FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) strategy is a powerful approach to long-term financial planning. It allows you to reach a point where your current investments can grow on their own to fund your retirement—without needing additional contributions. This Coast FIRE calculator helps you determine how your savings will grow over time and whether you're on track to achieve financial independence.

Instead of guessing or relying on complex spreadsheets, this tool simplifies the process by instantly calculating your future investment value based on your current savings, monthly contributions, expected annual return, and time horizon. Whether you're just starting your financial journey or optimizing an existing plan, this calculator provides clarity and direction.

Coast FIRE is ideal for individuals who want flexibility in their careers, reduced financial stress, and the ability to "coast" toward retirement once a certain savings milestone is reached. By understanding your numbers today, you can make smarter financial decisions for tomorrow.

How to Use

  1. Enter your current savings amount.
  2. Input your monthly contribution.
  3. Provide your expected annual return rate.
  4. Enter the number of years until retirement.
  5. Click "Calculate" to see your future value.
